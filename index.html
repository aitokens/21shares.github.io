21Shares & Stellar Blockchain

Institutional-Grade Access to the Future of Digital Assets

21Shares is a leading digital asset issuer specializing in regulated cryptocurrency exchange-traded products (ETPs). While currently focused on major blockchains like Bitcoin and Ethereum, 21Shares continues to explore innovative ecosystems — including the Stellar Blockchain — for future ETP development and tokenized asset integration.

Why Stellar?

The Stellar network is a decentralized, open-source blockchain designed for fast, low-cost cross-border payments and asset tokenization. With native support for stablecoins, fiat gateways, and smart contracts via Soroban, Stellar is emerging as a key player in institutional and financial-grade blockchain solutions.

Potential future 21Shares ETPs could include exposure to:
  • Stellar (XLM) – the native asset
  • Stellar-based stablecoins (e.g., USDC issued on Stellar)
  • Tokenized real-world assets (RWAs) on the Stellar network

21Shares: Compliance, Innovation & Multi-Chain Vision

Headquartered in Switzerland, 21Shares is known for its rigorous regulatory standards and institutional focus. As the digital asset landscape evolves, 21Shares is actively evaluating blockchain ecosystems like Stellar that align with its mission: delivering secure, transparent, and exchange-listed ETPs.

By partnering with regulated custodians and major exchanges, 21Shares ensures that any future product — whether based on Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Stellar-based assets — meets the highest standards of security and compliance.
